Some other details about ASB

You might be new to Discord and wonder what does a Bot do, So i will now tell you what a Bot does. A discord bot is a bot that runs only on discord, it provides with you more functionality than a normal user has, the best part is you can code your own bot and make it do whatever you want. A discord bot can be a 1. Chat Bot (a bot that chat & tries to behave like humans) 2. Moderation Bot ( it will automatically moderate your server; kick, mute or ban members, if someone tries to create spam, or anything similar) 3. Music Bot (It can play music in a Discord Server from Youtube or anywhere else) Bots are generally used for 1. Manage Servers (Kick, Ban Mute Members) via bot commands. 2. Control the level of Spam 3. Play Music right from within Discord. 4. View information about your Discord server or user. 5. Create Webhooks for your one or many Github Repositories or a site that supports it. 5. Create new features, that may not be already available on Discord. Ex> Levelling System The possibilities are endless, it depends on the developer what he have built.
